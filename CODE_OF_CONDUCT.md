# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want this project to be welcoming and respectful for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and professional.
- Assume good intent.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the project.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination of any kind.
- Trolling, intimidation, or deliberate disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Scope

This applies in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and related community channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and contributions that violate this code.

Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent bans from project participation.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers privately via GitHub (maintainer contact in this repository).

All reports will be reviewed and handled as promptly and fairly as possible.
